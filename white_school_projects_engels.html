School projects

At school we have many projects, both individually and in
groups. An example of this is creating a website. This can be
done in different ways, for example by designing a website
yourself with Figma, where you are completely free to think
of what kind of website you want to create. The project can
also be group-oriented, where you think of and develop a
website together with your team. The goal is to use your
creativity and technical skills to create a functional and
attractive website.
