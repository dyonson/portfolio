Business life

Business refers to all economic activities carried out by
companies, organizations and enterprises to produce, sell or
provide goods and services. It forms the commercial sector
of society, in which businesses operate to make profits while
promoting employment and economic growth.

Working for boss

Working for a boss means that you are employed by a
company or organization and are supervised by a manager or
employer. This is one of the most common forms of work,
where you offer your skills and time in exchange for salary
and benefits.
